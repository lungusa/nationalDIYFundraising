DIY Fundraising

The American Lung Association's DIY Fundraising platform enables you to build a custom web page where you can easily raise funds to support our cause. Then, you can invite others to donate to your personal fundraising campaign. Visitors can make personal donations and leave comments supporting your fundraising efforts.

Find a DIY Fundraising Page

If you have a friend or family member who has already created a DIY Fundraising page through the American Lung Association, use our search tool to find their unique fund.

Create a DIY Fundraising Page

Whether you have an event already scheduled, have one in planning, or simply want to create a fund to raise money and further the mission of the American Lung Association, we can help you get started with your personal fundraising page.
