To create a new fund or work with another fund you have created, click the Champion Dashboard link in the right corner.

From the links above, choose:

  • Fund Page to personalize the web page that tells your story about the person or cause you are championing for this fund, add photo albums, etc.
  • Address Book, Email, and Follow Up to access tools for easily contacting your family and friends for donation, and keeping track of people to personally thank.
  • Fund Summary to see how much money your fund has raised and who donated the money, as well as enter cash or checks people have given directly to you for the fund.

From the Fund Information area, choose:

  • Edit Fund Settings to edit the fund name or other details.
  • Create a Personalized Web Address to make an easy link for friends and family to remember.

*Note: Help links are available on each page to assist you with tasks.
